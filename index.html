About Us

About: A family-run flower farm in Somers, CT. We grow and sell fresh, seasonal flowers with love and care, from our land to your hands.

Mission Statement: At Pax Petals, our mission is to cultivate beauty, joy, and connection through sustainably grown flowers and heartfelt service. Rooted in our family values and the soil of Somers, CT, we are dedicated to offering fresh, seasonal blooms that brighten homes, celebrate life's precious moments, and foster a deeper appreciation for nature and community.
